Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

MoodRx is built on a simple principle: your mental health data belongs to you. Everything you log stays on your device. We do not sell, share, or monetize your personal information.

What We Collect

MoodRx stores the following data locally on your device only, using your device's secure local storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Mood check-ins (mood type, intensity score, timestamp)
  • Post-workout mood ratings
  • Supplement log entries
  • Session history and streaks
  • App preferences (notification settings, reminder times)

None of this data is transmitted to our servers or any third party. It exists only on your phone and is deleted when you use "Delete All Data" in Settings or uninstall the app.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any account information
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting SDKs that transmit personal data
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or sell data to advertisers
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not access your camera, microphone, or contacts

Payments & Subscriptions

MoodRx Pro subscriptions are processed through RevenueCat and the respective app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store). When you make a purchase:

  • Payment information is handled entirely by Apple or Google — we never see your card details
  • RevenueCat receives anonymized purchase receipts to validate your subscription status
  • RevenueCat may collect a randomly generated anonymous user ID and purchase history for subscription management

You can review RevenueCat's privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Push Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, MoodRx schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and do not involve any server or third-party service. You can disable them at any time in the app's Settings screen or in your device's notification settings.

Children's Privacy

MoodRx is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children. If you believe a child has used the app and you have concerns, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app. Continued use of MoodRx after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
